The European Economic Union (EEU)

Considered to be a major threat to the Earth Confederation, with the original confrontation in 2076, by the ESS Berlin. The EEU is determined to overthrow the Earth Confederation and replace it with a dictatorial monarchy. It's leaders are unknown, it's resources are unknown. The only remnant of the EEU is a small, isolated colony and military base on the surface of Venus, obscured from the EarthConfed and Earth Confederation Armed Forces by the violent storms on the planet they now call home.

Earth Confederation Armed Forces hypothesizes that the EEU may have five small destroyers in it's fleet, housed on the surface. Earth Confederation refuses to acknowledge the EEU to the public.
