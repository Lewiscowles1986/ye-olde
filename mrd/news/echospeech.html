"EchoSpeech" Speech Compression Free on the Web

CARPINTERIA, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--May 21, 1996--Echo Speech Corp. Tuesday announced that EchoSpeech, a high-quality voice-compression program used for adding speech to web pages, is now available at no charge to individuals, non-profit organizations and educational institutions.

Commercial sites may license EchoSpeech for just $99 per home page. EchoSpeech software can be implemented as a Netscape Navigator plug-in as well as a standalone speech utility for voice applications. Additional information (including free utilities, plug-ins and helper apps) is available at www.echospeech.com.

Echo Speech Corp. is a leader in developing DSP-based audio hardware and software as well as speech technology for the PC industry.

EchoSpeech voice-compression software is used in multimedia software, simultaneous voice and gaming utilities, Internet and intranet "telephones," voice messaging via e-mail, and educational programs. It is available for the Macintosh as well as Windows-based hardware.
