Power Systems

After the advent of fusion power, a new type of power relay network came into use, with backups and redundancies all over the station.

There are over fifty different fusion reactors powering Hope Station, half of them responsible for powering the long rotator 'cuff', which rotates the station to create a gravitational field. The other half is entrusted with providing power to the station in the form of life support and other needed functions.

Power relays are installed every five hundred meters of corridor, with a major junction point at every ten thousand. The power relay network is set up for easy access from a panel, or a crawlspace somewhere inside the station.
