Hope Station Timeline

1999: The Third World War breaks out after the situation in the Persian Gulf escalates to nclude the Allied Forces (United States, Canada, Meixco, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Spain, and Japan) and the recently formed Asian Defense Pact (Russia, Eastern Europe, Middle East). The United Nations is disbanded with the withdrawal of the nations who signed the Asian Defense Pact.
2004: IBM/Apple fund Project Widemind (AI project) for the next several decades.
2005: Third World War ends, with the Allied Forces the definitive victor. The world breaks up into Economic Unions. Japan and the United States, along with Canada and Mexico form the NorthWest Pacific Community (NWPC). All of Europe and part of western Russia form the European Economic Union (EEU). Eastern Russia is left to fend for itself, without any support from the EUs. The isolationistic continents of South America and Africa form the Atlantic Alliance (AA). Austrailia remains a self-sustaining nation.
2006: Austrailia joins with the Northwest Pacific Community, which renames itself to the Pacific Community (PC), to reflect the newest member. With this newfound support, the PC becomes immensely powerful, equalling itself with the European Economic Union.
2014: The EEU reinstates it's Space Program, launching the first shuttle flight in fifteen years. The Atlantic Alliance "retaliates" by putting two orbital defense platforms in orbit three months later.
2015: The eight members of the shuttle Mandela from the Atlantic Alliance are killed when the left solid rocket booster suffers thermal curtain failure, destroying the launch facility at Cape Horn. The AA subsequently shelves all future space shots. EEU authorities make plans to upgrad their space shuttle to a different design, to compensate for the design flaws the AA ran into. The Pacific Community follows suit.
2016: The IOC repeals drug testing from the Olympics. An althete from the AA suffers from an overdose and dies during the powerlifting event. Public outcry forces the IOC to reinstate mandatory drug testing.
2018: The PC makes the first landing on Mars. The EEU makes it's first landing on the Moon, and begins plans to construct the first Moon Colony.
2020: The first three-dimensional or 'Holovideo' film hits the theaters and become an instant success. The movie is "Star Trek: Dimensions".
2022: The EEU colony on the Moon is named Lunaport Colony, and the PC begins building the first space station, to be named Tranquility.
2024: New steel alloy (Reinforced Tritanium), which becomes a key element in spaceframes and ship hulls, is first manufactured on Lunaport Colony.
2025: Tranquility Station is completed, and subsequently sabotaged by terrorists, rumored to be affiliated with the Atlantic Alliance. Fourteen hundred PC and EEU citizens and military are killed, including the Vice-President of the PC and the Prime Minister of the EEU, as all life-support fails. The PC makes plans to continue the Tranquility Project with repairs to the current station, and one to be in orbit around Mars, to be named Freedom.
2026: The AA reopens Cape Horn, in launches two orbital defense platforms, this time, over the North American and European continents, in direct violation of the Treaty of St. Petersburg, signed at the end of World War III. The PC and the EEU send up defense platforms of their own, and unofficially enter into an uneasy alliance with each other, to defend themselves against the AA.
2030: The Atlantic Alliance begins making threats again Japan, just after the Japanese sciences perfect the first cybernetic organism or Cyborg. The PC refrains from commenting, enraging the AA even further.
2031: The Atlantic Alliance stakes Eastern Russia as it's own territory, the other two EUs protest, again citing the Treaty of St. Petersburg. The AA withdraws from the Treaty, stating it made no allowances for the growth of the Alliance. The EEU declares war on the Alliance, while the PC states neutrailty, and continues it's space program.
2032: The Battle of Sicily occurs. Casualties number in the tens of thousands on the side of the Atlantic Alliance, while the EEU takes only a few hundred. The AA withdraws from Sicily, and launches a thermonuclear weapon at the capital of the EEU, Paris. The EEU's orbital defense network easily destroys the weapon from orbit. Tranquility Station is reopened, Station Freedom around Mars is near completion.
2034: World War IV continues with both sides taking heavy losses. The PC finds itself living off the weapons making business, creating an unheard of amount of money for the PC. Station Freedom opens three years ahead of schedule. Plans for a third station in orbit of Jupiter are made by the PC, and the mining operation of the asteroid belt beyond Mars begins.
2035: The AA invades Paris, the EEU's Parliament is levelled by a geologic explosive. After a seven day battle, the AA is driven from Paris by EEU forces. The EEU makes a desperate plea to the Pacific Community: HELP! The PC maintains it's neutrality.
2036: The AA makes a strategical ambush on PC forces at a Defense Station in Mexico. The new Cyborgs make short work and successfully defends the station. The AA make sure to give the Pacific Community a wide berth. Seventy thousand EEU citizens 'immigrate' to the PC, and request transportation to the space stations. The PC begins mass transportation of both EEU and PC citizens to Tranquilty and Station Freedom. The EEU protests, but the PC maintains it's neutrality by offering the same option to the citizens of the AA.
2037: World War IV ends after the Battle of Bogota, which was fought to a standstill after two weeks of non-stop fighting. The Treaty of San Francisco is signed, the Pacific Community hosting the peace summit.
2042: Eastern Russia, then a part of the Atlantic Alliance, secedes from the AA in a surprise move. The leaders of that territory call themselves the Russian Combine, and impose strict borders against the PC and the EEU. The Pacific Community and the European Economic Union formally recognize the Russian Combine, the Atlantic Alliance declares war upon the newest EU, and inform the PC and the EEU to remain neutral. World War V begins when the Russian Combine attacks and destroys four EEU cities along the western border, claiming they were Alliance strongholds. The EEU declares war on the Russian Combine. Four months later, the Pacific Community closes it's borders and continues to adopt it's isolationist policy it had during the last World War. PC Space Station Liberty opens in orbit around Jupiter.
2045: World War V ends. The Russian Combine continues to survive, the EEU and AA agree to cease fire, due to low resources. The Alliance formerly recognizes the Russian Combine. Cyborg rights are estabilshed in the Pacific Community.
2049: The first trial involving a Cyborg occurs when he is charged with a crime (murder). He is cleared of the charges and evidence proves later on that he was framed by anti-cyborg political group.
2051: The European Economic Union elects it's first Cyborg Prime Minister. The EEU becomes isolationist, seperating itself from the rest of the planet. The Alliance enters into a mutual defense pact with the Russian Combine and the Pacific Community, called the Asian-American Alliance (AAA).
2060: AAA Space Colony Jericho is established on the surface of Mars. The EEU's stellar activites become classified, and all AAA citizens are ejected from Lunaport Colony. The PC, AA, and RC embassies are closed and non EEU citizens are ordered to leave or be killed. The AAA begins withdrawing it's citizens, and the EEU is free of "aliens" in less than a month.
2063: Pacific Community President Jennifer Kyoto is assasinated by unknown individuals, during her tour of Station Freedom. The EEU is rumored to have a part in the assassination, but no conclusions are made, and the matter remains unsolved. Vice-President Mariposa Mendez becomes the Pacific Community's twentieth President, the first President to gain office by means other than popular elections.
2069: President Mendez orders the space program to be formed to a new branch of military service, and the Pacific Community's Earth Confederation Armed Forces is formed, charged with the protection of Earth. The AAA gives the Pacific Community full support, the EEU continues to isolate itself.
2070: The PCSC Confederation is launched, the first of ten 'planned' defensive space cruisers. After it's successful trial runs, the PCSC Tokyo, and the PCSC British Columbia are launched, six months later.
2075: The AAA endures, until the Atlantic Alliance withdraws from the defense accords after the EEU begins enforcing it's borders in naval conflicts, and takes matters into it's own hands. The Asian/American Alliance disbands, and Mars Colony Jericho splits into three segments (Lick, Fontana, and Gerrold). Tranquility Station is dismantled, immediately after Tranquility Two opens.
2076: Tranquility Two is completely destroyed by a direct hit from the unknown ESS Berlin. Stellar War ensues, with three new Pacific Community ships constructed. The Stellar War lasts for seven days, and the ESS Berlin is destroyed, just after destroying Station Freedom. This marks the first planetary conflict, that did not occur on the planet, but in space. Not a single shot was fired on the surface.
2077: The Pacific Community launches the PCSS Agamemnon, the first spaceship with the experimental fusion drive, allowing the ship to accelerate far beyond that of the curent Confederation class of space cruiser.
2080: The Russian Combine joins the Pacific Community.
2087: Citizens of the EEU and Alliance overthrow their governments. Both regions are destabilized for a period of three years.
2089: Major League Baseball expands to an unprecedented sixty-one teams, spanning four leagues and eight divisions. Amongst them are the Lick Segment Miners (Jericho Colony, Mars), and the West Lunaport Devildogs. First Weightless Ballet performed in Space at Tranquility.
2090: In a bold move by the Pacific Community, it opens it's borders to the rest of the world, and proposes a complete unification of the planet. Happy to finally have direction and purpose, the rest of the world accepts, and the Earth Confederation is formed, with the Pacific Community's Earth Confederation Armed Forces continuing to defend the planet. Plans for Hope Station are approved and construction begins. First "spacer" (spaceborn) author, Ianni McAllister, publishes her book, "Jaunting Across the Sun."
2091: The first time an interplanetary expansion team wins a league pennant, the Fontana Segment Saucers from Jericho Colony, Mars. They lose the Planetary Series to the San Jose Giants by a close 4 games to 3.
2096: Hope Station is completed, and Earth Confederation Armed Forces's Station Command division is placed in command of the station, which has a maximum capacity of one million people.
