  • Important People: The commander of the station is Rear Admiral Maximillian Andrews, and his executive officer is Commander Broc MacEwen. The Earth Confederation's president is Jacquelin Otonashi, and the leader of the Lunaport Autonimous Zone is Carter Nunez.

  • Security Measures: Projectile weapons are not allowed on the station, due to the possibility of hull breach, and security patrols happen on a regular schedule -- much like beat cops in a large city.

  • Hope Station's commerce hub is a center of activity, similar to the downtown section of a large city. Other ares include the Residential hub, Docking hub, and Ops hub, which houses a hospital and restricted command areas.

  • Technology: Most paper products have been replaced by flat handheld computers called scribers. They feature a thin, high-resolution screen that accepts both voice and stylus input.

  • Communications: Station residents carry 'commos;' small communications devices capable of both audio and visual transmission, using the small lens to display a life-sized image of the person speaking. Commo transmissions can be routed to scribers, terminals, and other video devices.

  • Travel: Travel from Earth to Hope Station takes at least two months each way. Jericho Colony on Mars is five weeks away, while Vulcan and Esiban are a mere two-hour jump from the station.
