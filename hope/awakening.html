Emerging from an age marked by war and frantic exploration, humanity is poised on the brink of the twenty-second century. With colonies and stations scattered across the solar system, and the echoes of civil war still ringing in men's ears, humankind prepares to heal its wounds and enter a new dawn. At the edge of explored space, we must grapple with age-old struggles... awakening to new horizons as we discover that we are not alone in the universe. The date is 2099. The place is Hope Station...

 

 

Hope Station: Awakening is a roleplaying theme on The Nexus, one of the internet's premier RP MOOs. A MOO, according to The MUD Connector, is "an environment where multiple people may be logged on and interacting with one another." Like most role-playing MOOs, Awakenings uses the environment as a sort of 'stage' for improvisational acting and storytelling in a science fiction setting. Players create characters and interact "in-character" with each other, creating new plots and adventures as they go.
